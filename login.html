Reduce Fine Lines

It is time to utilize our technological advances and stop having to worry about such trivial information such as food expiration dates. With Simply Unused the user can input their groceries into their own personal account.

Save The Date

The information stored will be that of the quantity, expiration date, and a photo of the food product. Upon adding their items this information is then saved into the “pantry” where items can be organized by name, quantity, or expiration date.

DM

As an addition to storing this information this website will alert the user by discoloring the food card and prioritizing it in the pantry. This will also help reduce wastes that comes from unused groceries decreasing carbon footprint.
